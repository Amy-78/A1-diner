A True Historic Landmark

Step back in time to 1946 inside our beautifully preserved, authentic O'Mahony diner car suspended gracefully above the street. As a designated historic landmark in the heart of Gardiner, Maine, our cozy and nostalgic atmosphere surrounds you with gleaming chrome, classic booths, and rich community memories. It is more than just a place to enjoy a home-cooked meal; it is a living piece of New England history where every corner tells a story.

The Heart of Maine Dining

The Full Diner Experience

Whether you are looking for a quick bite or a relaxed family meal, we accommodate every preference with our versatile dining options. Choose from cozy dine-in counter service, traditional booth table service, a welcoming outdoor seating area, or fast curbside pickup and takeout options for those on the move. We are proudly family-friendly, LGBTQ+ friendly, and dog-friendly on our outdoor patio, ensuring a warm and inclusive welcome for everyone in your crew.
