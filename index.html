A True Historic Landmark

A-1 Diner is proudly housed inside a beautifully preserved, authentic suspended railroad car that fills the air with nostalgic charm. We bring old-school heritage to life daily through a warm, classic diner atmosphere built on community, full flavor, and unforgettable comfort food. Step inside to relax, reminisce, and experience authentic Maine history at its best.

The Full Diner Experience

Enjoy multi-faceted service built around your routine: step inside for classic cozy dine-in counter service, comfortable table service, or grab your favorites via rapid takeout and curbside pickup options.
