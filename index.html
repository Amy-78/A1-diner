A True Historic Landmark

Housed inside a beautifully preserved, authentic suspended railroad car, the A-1 Diner offers a step back into a classic era of dining. Originally built in the mid-20th century, our diner retains its cozy, historic atmosphere characterized by iconic polished chrome and warm wood paneling. We pride ourselves on keeping this community landmark vibrant, welcoming regulars and newcomers alike under our original vintage roof.

The Full Diner Experience

We provide a versatile range of dining styles to fit your day, including cozy dine-in counter service, comfortable table seating, and a welcoming outdoor area for clear days. If you are on the move, we offer fast curbside pickup and convenient takeout options. Our dining room is proudly family-friendly and LGBTQ+ friendly, and we are happy to welcome your dogs in our designated outdoor seating space.
