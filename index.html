THE HERITAGE

Honest cooking crafted with wood, iron, and fire.

At The Iron & Oak, our culinary philosophy centers on clean, robust flavor profiles brought to life over open flames. Guided by historical tavern recipes and locally sourced Colorado ingredients, we dry-age our meats and premium cuts, using genuine oak and hickory woods.

Every meal is served in cast iron skillets or over local slate to lock in optimal heat and flavor. From slow-smoked ribs to signature bison burgers, we invite you to sit back and experience rustic, premium dining.
