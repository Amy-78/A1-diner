THE PHILOSOPHY

Fresh sea breeze, organic soils, culinary craft.

Aura & Wave was founded to honor the exquisite dialogue between sun-warmed organic landscapes and the clean salty depths of the ocean. Each afternoon, we partner directly with independent deep-sea fishermen and coastal organic farmers to source dynamic, pure ingredients.

We preserve raw flavors using simple high-heat techniques—blistering on salt plates, smoking over aromatic olive woods, and pairing with house-pressed olive oils and hand-harvested citrus segments.
